FAQs

On this page you'll find a list of frequently asked questions.

If you cannot find an answer to your question, you can submit a query below and we will get back to you as soon as possible.




How do I import my timetable?

You can import yout timetable to our site using the URL generated when you subscribe to your universty timetable, you just input it to our website and we'll take it from there!

How do I input my tasks?

You can input your tasks through the To-Do-list page, where you'll have the option to add the deadline for the given task, you can mark it as pending or finished, and you can choose to import it to the timetable or just keep it in the list.

What do you do with my location data?

You will get your reminders through your email. You'll get your class check-in reminders 10 min before your class starts and your deadline reminders 24hrs before the deadline.

Who is it for?

The website currently only caters for Computer Science students at the University of Manchester.

How frequently do I have to update my university timetable?

Unless there have been changes to your university schedule, your timetable will be fixed and only requires one import at the beginnging of the semester!
