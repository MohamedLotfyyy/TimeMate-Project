AUTOMATICALLY ORGANIZES

The main aim of our website is to help you with organizing your day-to-day tasks from University work to your personal outings, that is done automatically using our algorithm which will automatically sort all your activities into the timetable.

REMINDERS

Another tool that will help you manege your time well and stay on top of your assignments is reminders, specifically for your deadlines and classes. You will get them through your email, 24hrs before your assignments deadlines, and 10 min before you need to check in for your class.

MONTHLY RUNDOWNS

The monthly rundown will contain all the deadlines that you have due for the whole month week by week. Each week will showcase how many deadlines you have, and further information regarding these deadlines.
